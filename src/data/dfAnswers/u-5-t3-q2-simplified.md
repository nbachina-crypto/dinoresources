**Network acquisition** means collecting evidence from **network traffic and network devices**.
This includes capturing:
* **packets**
* **IP addresses**
* **log files**
* **router logs**
* **server access records**
The main challenge is that network data is **temporary** and can disappear quickly if not captured live.
Other challenges include:
* **large traffic volume**
* **encrypted data**
* **packet loss**
* **different timestamps across devices**
It is important for **tracing communication** and **investigating network-related incidents**.
