**Ontologies** in mobile and embedded forensics are **structured models** used to organize digital evidence and show relationships between different types of data.
They help classify information such as:
* **messages**
* **call logs**
* **device details**
* **timestamps**
* **system logs**
For example, they can show that:
* a **message belongs to a user account**
* a **log entry is linked to a specific time**
This helps investigators connect evidence from **mobile phones** and **embedded devices** like **CCTV systems** or **routers** in a clear and systematic way.
