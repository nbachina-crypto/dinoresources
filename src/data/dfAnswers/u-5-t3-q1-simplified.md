### What is Local Acquisition?
**Local acquisition** means collecting digital evidence directly from a device **without moving it**.
---
### Why It Is Used
* To capture important data while the system is still running
* To prevent loss of **volatile data** like memory and active processes
---
### Example
* Copying **RAM data**
* Creating a **disk image** while the system is on
---
### Important Point
> Interacting with a live system can change some data, so it must be handled carefully.
---
### Final Idea
* Collect evidence **on-site**
* Capture **live data quickly**
* Ensure **careful handling to preserve evidence integrity**
