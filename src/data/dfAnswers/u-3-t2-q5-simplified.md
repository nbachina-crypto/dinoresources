**Data carving** is a method used to **recover deleted or lost files directly from raw disk data**.
It does not depend on the file system. Instead, it looks for known **file signatures**, such as the starting and ending patterns of file types like **JPEG** or **PDF**.
This helps investigators recover files even when:
* **file names**
* **folders**
* **metadata**
are deleted.
It is commonly used to recover **deleted photos, documents, and videos** from **unallocated space** or **damaged disks** during forensic investigations.
