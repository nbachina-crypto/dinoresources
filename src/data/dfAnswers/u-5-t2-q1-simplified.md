Tracing information on the Internet means finding **where online activity came from** and **how it moved across networks**.
Common techniques include checking:
* **IP addresses**
* **log files**
* **DNS records**
* **email headers**
Investigators also analyze network **packets** to trace communication between devices and servers.
These methods help identify:
* **user activity**
* **website access history**
* **email origins**
* **communication paths on the Internet**
