**Timeline analysis** means arranging digital events in **time order** to understand what happened on a device.
It uses:
* **timestamps**
* **log files**
* **file details**
to track actions such as:
* **file creation**
* **modification**
* **deletion**
* **logins**
* **device connections**
For example, it helps show **when a file was created, opened, and deleted**.
This is important because it helps investigators rebuild the **sequence of events** and understand **user actions clearly**.
It is widely used to find evidence in cases like:
* **data deletion**
* **unauthorized access**
* **file transfer investigations**
