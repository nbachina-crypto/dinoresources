**Mobile devices and embedded systems** are treated as digital evidence when they contain useful investigation data.
### Mobile Devices
Mobile phones can provide:
* **messages**
* **call history**
* **photos**
* **app data**
* **location details**
### Embedded Systems
Embedded systems such as:
* **CCTV devices**
* **routers**
* **GPS units**
can provide:
* **log files**
* **timestamps**
* **activity records**
In both cases, investigators first **secure the device**, create a **forensic copy**, verify it using a **hash value**, and then analyze the copied data.
The **original device is preserved** to maintain **evidence integrity** and **chain of custody**.
