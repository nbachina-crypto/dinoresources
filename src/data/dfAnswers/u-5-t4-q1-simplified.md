In **network forensics**, the **examination phase** means checking collected **packet data** and **log files** to extract useful details like:
* **IP addresses**
* **ports**
* **protocols**
* **timestamps**
The **analysis phase** means interpreting this information to understand **what happened on the network**.
This includes:
* **rebuilding communication sessions**
* **arranging events in time order**
* **tracing traffic source and destination**
These phases help investigators **identify suspicious activity** and **reconstruct network events clearly**.
