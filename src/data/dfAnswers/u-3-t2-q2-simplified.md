**Analysis tools** are software used in digital forensics to examine digital evidence safely. They help investigators study copied data **without changing the original device**.
These tools can:
* open a **disk image**
* recover **deleted files**
* check **hash values**
* read file systems like **FAT** and **NTFS**
* analyze **log files**
They also help rebuild **timelines** by showing when files were:
* **created**
* **modified**
* **deleted**
Examples include:
* **Autopsy**
* **FTK**
* **EnCase**
* **Wireshark**
* **Cellebrite**
Their main role is to **find evidence, preserve its integrity, and prepare reports for investigation and legal use**.
