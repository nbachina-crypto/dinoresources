The **examination phase in mobile forensics** is the process of analyzing the data collected from the mobile device.
Investigators work on the **forensic copy** and check:
* **messages**
* **call history**
* **contacts**
* **photos**
* **app data**
* **browser history**
* **location details**
They also recover **deleted files** and examine **timestamps** and **log files**.
This phase helps understand **what actions were performed on the device and when they happened**.
