**Embedded systems** are devices like **CCTV units, routers, smart TVs, and GPS systems** that contain built-in computers.
In forensics, their **logs and stored data** are examined.
**Mobile phone forensics** involves recovering data such as:
* **calls**
* **messages**
* **photos**
* **app data**
* **deleted files**
from phones.
**Telecommunication network forensics** deals with communication records like:
* **call logs**
* **tower data**
* **SMS records**
from service providers.
All three help investigators **collect digital evidence** and **rebuild user activity and communication history**.
