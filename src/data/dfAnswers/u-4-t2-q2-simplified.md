In **mobile forensics**, **malware** means harmful software present on a mobile device, such as:
* **spyware**
* **trojans**
* **malicious apps**
It may:
* **steal data**
* **track users**
* **send messages**
* **delete evidence**
Investigators check:
* **installed apps**
* **logs**
* **permissions**
* **background processes**
to detect malware activity.
**Security is important** because the device must be protected from:
* **network changes**
* **remote wiping**
* **further infection**
So, the device is **isolated**, a **forensic copy** is created, and analysis is done on the **copy instead of the original**.
