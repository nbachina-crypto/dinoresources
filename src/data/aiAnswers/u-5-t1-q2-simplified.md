* **Conditional Probability** → chance of one event when another event is already known
* **Full Joint Distribution** → table of all possible combinations and their probabilities
Example:
* disease and fever = **0.15**
* fever = **0.30**
Then:
**P(Disease|Fever) = 0.15 / 0.30 = 0.5**
So the chance of disease becomes **50%**.
Example of full joint distribution:
| Rain | Traffic | Probability |
| ---- | ------- | ----------- |
| Yes  | High    | 0.30        |
| Yes  | Low     | 0.10        |
| No   | High    | 0.20        |
| No   | Low     | 0.40        |
It is important in **AI** because **all other probabilities can be calculated from it**.
