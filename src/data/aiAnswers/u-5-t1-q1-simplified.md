**Acting under uncertainty** means making decisions when **all information is not known**.
This is necessary because real-world situations are often **unclear or unpredictable**.
### Examples
* fog in self-driving cars
* medical diagnosis with incomplete symptoms
* games involving random events
### Main Challenges
* **incomplete data**
* **uncertain results**
* **sensor errors**
* **changing environment**
AI handles this using **probability and logical reasoning** to choose the **best possible action**.
