Bayes Rule is used in **AI** to update the chance of something being true after getting new information.
Formula:
**P(A|B) = (P(B|A) × P(A)) / P(B)**
Simple example:
Initial chance of disease = **10%**
After observing a symptom, the system recalculates the probability.
The updated chance becomes **40%**.
This means the **AI system improves its decision using new evidence**.
Applications include:
* spam email detection
* medical diagnosis
* speech recognition
* robot navigation
* image classification
It is mainly used when **AI systems must make decisions with uncertain data**.
