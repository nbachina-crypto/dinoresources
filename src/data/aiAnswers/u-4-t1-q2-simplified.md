`Forward Chaining` starts from known facts and moves forward to find conclusions.
Example:
```text id="18427"
fact -> rule -> conclusion
```
`Backward Chaining` starts from the goal and works backward to check whether the facts support it.
Example:
```text id="92534"
goal -> required fact -> verify fact
```
Difference:
* forward = fact-based
* backward = goal-based
Both are used for logical reasoning in AI systems.
