In **First-Order Logic**, a **model** gives meaning to statements by defining the **objects and their properties**.
### Example
Human(Socrates)
This is true if the model contains **Socrates as a human**.
**Quantifiers** are used to talk about **all or some objects**.
* **∀** means **for all**
  Example: all humans are mortal
* **∃** means **there exists**
  Example: there exists a student
So:
* **model = meaning and truth**
* **quantifier = scope of objects in the statement**
