**Adversarial Search** is an AI technique used in games where **two players compete against each other**.
It works by checking **all possible moves** and the opponent’s possible responses before choosing the **best move**.
The most common method is **Minimax**, where:
* one player tries to **maximize the score**
* the other tries to **minimize it**
This helps the AI **think ahead** and play intelligently in **2-player games** like chess and tic-tac-toe.
**Alpha-Beta Pruning** is used to make the search faster by **skipping unnecessary moves**.
