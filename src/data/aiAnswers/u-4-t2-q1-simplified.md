**First-Order Logic (FOL)** is used to represent **facts about objects and relationships**.
### Syntax
Syntax means **how the statement is written**.
Example:
∀x ,(Human(x) → Mortal(x))
This follows FOL rules.
### Semantics
Semantics means **what the statement means**.
The above statement means:
> **Every human is mortal.**
So:
* **syntax = form**
* **semantics = meaning**
FOL is used in AI for **logical reasoning and knowledge representation**.
